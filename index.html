Essential Queer Cinema

The following 16 films show the breadth of diversity in the Queer experience. Whether it be toxic lovers trying to repair their relationship via relocation, undocumented refugees trying to navigate multiple identies, or the joy in building a space for your community, there are so many different stories worth experiencing. While all of these may not be explicitly queer, the focus is on queer characters, queer directors, or documentaries about queer people.
